Printing Turn-Around Time

Turnaround Time is the time it takes to manufacture your order from your APPROVED print ready files. There are only 3 ways to start the clock ticking...
1. Submit your order with a 'NO PROOF' option. WE STRONGLY SUGGEST THAT YOU ORDER A PROOF. IF ANYTHING GOES WRONG WITH YOUR ORDER YOU ACCEPT FULL RESPONSIBILITY. Please read our Terms and Conditions for clarification.

2. Approve your PDF PROOF from your email confirmation.

3. Approve your HARD COPY PROOF from your fax attached sheet to our Customer Service Department.

TURNAROUND TIMES ALWAYS START AFTER PROOF APPROVAL.
Turnaround Time is calculated in BUSINESS DAYS ONLY AND EXCLUDES HOLIDAYS AND WEEKENDS.
WEEKEND DAYS and HOLIDAY JOB SUBMISSIONS START THE FOLLOWING BUSINESS DAY!
Proofing Turnaround Times:
Allow 48 hours for PDF proofs to be linked to your account
Allow 48 hours for your hard copy proof to ship
