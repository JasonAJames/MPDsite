JOOMLA XTREME LOCATOR COMPONENT

June 2007 :: Version 1.0

Placing a dealer locator on your Joomla website is easy.

  1. Download the component from http://www.iqservices.com/xlocator/joomla.html and install it from your Joomla component installation screen.
  2. Now sign up for a free trial of XtremeLocator at http://www.iqservices.com/xlocator/trial.html and set up your account. You’ll get an online control panel where you can list your dealers, and you can work with the look and feel of the locator there, too. Once you’re done, generate your new dealer locator’s iFrame code and copy that to your computer’s clipboard.
  3. Return to your Joomla admin panel, go to the components list, open the ExtremeLocator component, and then paste your code into the box marked "code".
  4. Now just link to the component from a menu item to display the dealer locator on your site. If necessary, tweak the component’s size settings to match your template’s width.
