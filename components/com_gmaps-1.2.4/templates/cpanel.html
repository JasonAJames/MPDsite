GMaps 1.0

GMaps enables you to create and manage your maps through a Joomla user interface versus having to hardcode the latitude/longitude values directly within your mambot (or plugin). You can also create composite maps based on markers from multiple maps (up to 5 -- will change in the future).

You will need to define all of your markers first. After all have been defined, then you can create your maps. After your map has been saved, you can then easily add/remove markers to the map or define map overlays.

For questions, comments/feedback or concerns, please go to the GMaps website and post the item on our forums. Documentation can also be found in the FAQ section of this site. (NOTE: The docs are unfinished but will be updated as timer permits)
